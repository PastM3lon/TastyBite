Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 800 g beef chuck (or short ribs)
  • 1 dried guajillo chili
  • 1 dried ancho chili
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 500 ml beef broth
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Tortillas (corn or flour)
  • Chopped cilantro and lime wedges (for serving)
  • Shredded cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Soak the dried chilis in hot water for 10-15 minutes until soft.
  2. Remove stems and seeds from chilis and blend with garlic, onion, cumin, oregano, paprika, and some beef broth into a smooth paste.
  3. Season the beef with salt and pepper and place it in a pot or slow cooker.
  4. Pour the chili sauce over the beef and add the bay leaf and remaining broth.
  5. Simmer on low heat for 2.5-3 hours (or slow cook) until the meat is tender and shreddable.
  6. Remove the beef and shred with two forks.
  7. Heat tortillas briefly in a pan, add shredded beef (and cheese, if using), and fold into tacos.
  8. Serve with chopped cilantro, lime wedges, and the cooking juices on the side for dipping.
