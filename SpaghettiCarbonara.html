Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 160-220 g spaghetti
  • 75 g pancetta or guanciale (or bacon), chopped
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 egg (or 2 egg yolks)
  • 25-50 g pecorino or parmesan cheese, grated
  • Black pepper
  • Salt (for the pasta water)

Instructions:

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti until al dente.
  2. In a bowl, whisk the egg with the grated cheese and black pepper.
  3. Place the pancetta and garlic clove in a cold pan and heat on medium.
  4. Cook until the pancetta becomes crispy and releases its fat.
  5. Remove the garlic clove from the pan.
  6. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan with the pancetta.
  7. Add a small amount of pasta cooking water.
  8. Turn off the heat and pour in the egg and cheese mixture.
  9. Toss the pasta quickly so the eggs form a creamy sauce.
  10. Serve with extra cheese and black pepper.
