Shawarma

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Fresh herbs
  • Vegetables (such as tomatoes or greens)
  • Pasta or main base ingredient
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Grated cheese

Instructions:

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil if cooking pasta.
  2. Prepare the ingredients by chopping garlic and vegetables.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant.
  5. Add the vegetables and cook until softened.
  6. Add the cooked pasta or main ingredient to the pan.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper.
  8. Toss everything together until well combined.
  9. Serve the dish and top with grated cheese and herbs.
