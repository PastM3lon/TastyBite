Shawarma

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg boneless chicken thighs (or breasts)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 6 pita breads (or wraps)
  • Fresh vegetables (lettuce, tomato, cucumber)
  • Garlic sauce or tahini (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, cumin, paprika, turmeric, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and coat them well.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (or overnight for best flavor).
  4. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F), or heat a grill/skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. Place marinated chicken on a baking tray or grill pan and cook until fully done (about 25-40 minutes).
  6. Remove chicken and slice it into thin strips.
  7. Warm the pita breads or wraps.
  8. Assemble wraps: spread sauce, add chicken, and top with vegetables.
  9. Fold or roll the wrap and serve immediately.
