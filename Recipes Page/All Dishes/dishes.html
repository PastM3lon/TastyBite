Spaghetti Carbonara

A creamy Roman pasta made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • 160-220 g spaghetti
  • 75 g pancetta or guanciale (or bacon), chopped
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 egg (or 2 egg yolks)
  • 25-50 g pecorino or parmesan cheese, grated
  • black pepper
  • salt (for pasta water)

Instructions

  1. Step 1
    Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Step 2
    In a bowl, whisk the egg with the grated cheese and black pepper.
  3. Step 3
    Place pancetta and garlic in a cold pan, then heat on medium.
  4. Step 4
    Cook until pancetta is crisp and has released its fat.
  5. Step 5
    Remove garlic, add cooked spaghetti, and toss well.
  6. Step 6
    Add a splash of pasta water for silkiness.
  7. Step 7
    Turn off heat, add egg-cheese mixture, and toss quickly.
  8. Step 8
    Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper.

Shawarma

Juicy spiced chicken wrapped in warm pita with fresh vegetables and sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg boneless chicken thighs (or breasts)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • salt and black pepper
  • 6 pita breads (or wraps)
  • fresh vegetables (lettuce, tomato, cucumber)
  • garlic sauce or tahini (optional)

Instructions

  1. Step 1
    Mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, cumin, paprika, turmeric, salt, and pepper.
  2. Step 2
    Add chicken and coat thoroughly in the marinade.
  3. Step 3
    Cover and chill at least 1 hour (overnight is best).
  4. Step 4
    Cook in oven or skillet until chicken is fully done.
  5. Step 5
    Slice chicken into thin strips.
  6. Step 6
    Warm pita breads or wraps.
  7. Step 7
    Assemble with sauce, chicken, and vegetables.
  8. Step 8
    Fold and serve warm.

Tacos

Tender beef tacos with bold chili flavors, served with lime and fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • 800 g beef chuck (or short ribs)
  • 1 dried guajillo chili
  • 1 dried ancho chili
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 500 ml beef broth
  • salt and black pepper
  • tortillas (corn or flour)
  • chopped cilantro and lime wedges
  • shredded cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Step 1
    Soak dried chilis in hot water until softened.
  2. Step 2
    Blend chilis with garlic, onion, cumin, oregano, paprika, and broth.
  3. Step 3
    Season beef and place in pot or slow cooker.
  4. Step 4
    Add chili sauce, bay leaf, and remaining broth.
  5. Step 5
    Simmer until beef is tender and shreddable.
  6. Step 6
    Shred beef with two forks.
  7. Step 7
    Warm tortillas and fill with beef and toppings.
  8. Step 8
    Serve with lime and fresh cilantro.
